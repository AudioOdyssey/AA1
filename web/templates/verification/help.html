Story Verification Instructions


AudioOdyssey, Inc. strives to present a safe and (moderately) appropriate gaming experience for our users. For this reason we require that all of our stories be verified by our team. We hope you find this page helpful and feel free to use the contact page to ask us any additional questions about our mission!

1) Select a story from the list:

Select a story by clicking on the title to open its verification form.

2) Review each Location, Object, Event and Decision:

Use the dropdowns to review all the relevant story information. Check for offensive content

Fields you must fill out on each verification form:

Locations Verified: Check this box to verify locations
Decisions Verified: Check this box to verify decisions
Objects Verified: Check this box to verify objects/body>
Events Verified: Check this box to verify events
Suggested Rating: Rate this story as you would a movie
Name of Verifier: Enter your first and last name
Today's date: Enter the date on which you verfied the story
Select the Submit Story Info button to submit this information.

Thank You for your assistance!
