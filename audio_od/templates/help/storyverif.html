Verification and Publication Information


All stories must be verified before they are available for purchase. Any aspect of your story which is not verified will be returned to you with comments and can be viewed by selecting the view verification information button located on the bottom of the story page. Additionally, verification information will appear on your dashboard. Only submit a story for verification once it is complete. Once your story has been verified it will show up in your "verified" stories on the menu bar on the left hand side of your dashboard. Here you can submit it for publication to the app and your story will then be available for purchase on the app. Once it is published it will show up in your published stories.


Thank you for your interest! We look forward to experiencing your AudioOdyssey!
